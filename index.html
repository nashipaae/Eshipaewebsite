About Eshipae lil heaven.

Welcome to our taste of heaven. Explore the natural beauty and simple joys you get when at Eshipae resort located at Seriani in Kajiado County..

  • Mission To provide genuine heartfelt hospitality, that will engrave a unique experience in the heart and mind of every guest.
  • Vision To be the guests’ first choice, by aiming to create everlasting memories through an unmatched personalised service.
  • Values We are guided, in our every action, by moral principle which are inspired by perpetual and unfading values

Our endeavours are sustained by a passionate, gracious team of individuals, committed to enhance the formulation of long-lasting, cherishing memories through a well beyond the ordinary, flawless service. This is a vital ingredient on which we focus heavily: Τhe proper development and flourishing of our people. We accomplish our goal by recognizing and respecting each other's uniqueness, acknowledging and appreciating each member's input, as well as treating everyone equally. We take under consideration that our company's prosperity, excellence and expansion are based on the well being, goodwill and unity of its people.
